Overview

This project focuses on integrating a seamless shopping experience within TikTok, enabling businesses to sell products directly through video content. Key features include product catalog integration, mobile-optimized interfaces, secure payment systems, and real-time analytics. The goal is to increase user engagement and sales through targeted marketing and influencer campaigns. Target Outcome: Successful launch with fully operational TikTok Shop, optimized for performance and scalability.
